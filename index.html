About Us

Yoga Chittagong - Your Premier Yoga Studio

Yoga Chittagong is a leading yoga studio in Chattogram, dedicated to providing authentic yoga experiences that nurture both body and mind. Our experienced instructors guide students of all levels through traditional yoga practices, helping them achieve balance, strength, and inner peace.

Located in the heart of Halishahar, we offer a serene environment perfect for your yoga journey. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced practitioner, our classes are designed to meet your individual needs and goals.

Our philosophy centers on the ancient wisdom of yoga combined with modern understanding of health and wellness. We believe that yoga is not just exercise, but a way of life that brings harmony to all aspects of being.

Join us in cultivating mindfulness, building strength, and finding your center amidst the bustling city of Chattogram.

Our Services

Hatha Yoga

Traditional yoga practice focusing on physical postures and breathing techniques. Perfect for beginners and those seeking gentle strengthening.

Vinyasa Flow

Dynamic, flowing sequences that build heat and strength while synchronizing movement with breath. Ideal for intermediate practitioners.

Meditation & Mindfulness

Learn various meditation techniques and mindfulness practices to cultivate mental clarity and emotional balance.

Private Sessions

One-on-one yoga instruction tailored to your specific needs, goals, and physical condition. Personalized attention for faster progress.

Yoga Therapy

Therapeutic yoga sessions designed to address specific health concerns, injuries, or chronic conditions under professional guidance.

Workshops & Retreats

Specialized workshops on advanced techniques, philosophy, and weekend retreats for deeper immersion in yoga practice.
